8. Children's Privacy (Mixed Audience)

Huntix is a mixed-audience app. Users of all ages may use the core game features. However, the following restrictions apply to protect younger users:

  • Under 13: Social features (chat, friends, trading) are fully disabled. Interstitial and rewarded ads are blocked. Only child-directed banner ads are shown (COPPA-compliant, content rating "G").
  • 13–17: Social features available with moderation tools. All ad types shown with age-appropriate content.
  • 18+: Full access to all features.

We use a neutral age gate (birth year) at first launch. Until age is verified, the app defaults to the most restrictive settings (child-directed). Ad requests for users under 13 are tagged as child-directed per COPPA requirements.

If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13 without appropriate safeguards, contact privacy@intelligame.app and we will promptly delete the data.

9. Advertising & Consent

Huntix uses Google AdMob to display ads. Ad types include banners, interstitials, and opt-in rewarded videos.

  • Under 13: Only banner ads with child-directed treatment (COPPA). No interstitials or rewarded ads.
  • EU/EEA users: A consent dialog (Google UMP SDK) is shown on first launch per GDPR.
  • VIP subscribers: All ads are removed.
